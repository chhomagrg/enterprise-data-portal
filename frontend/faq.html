Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions asked about our platform and services. Use the search or categories to find specific questions quickly.

What is the Enterprise Data Access Portal?

The Enterprise Data Access Portal is a platform designed to provide real-time insights and data related to solar energy generation...

How do I contact customer support?

If you need assistance, you can contact us through the 'Contact Us' link in the footer...

How do I log in to the platform?

To log in, simply click the 'Login' button on the top right corner...

How can I track solar energy data?

Once logged in, you can access solar energy data in the 'Data' section of the portal...

How do I change my password?

To change your password, go to the 'Account Settings' page, click on 'Change Password', and follow the prompts to set a new one.

Is there a mobile app for the portal?

Currently, there is no dedicated mobile app. However, the portal is mobile-responsive, so you can access it from any mobile device's browser.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account by contacting customer support. They will guide you through the account deletion process.

How can I update my email address?

You can update your email address by going to the 'Account Settings' section and modifying your contact details.

Can I analyze historical energy production data?

Yes, the portal provides access to historical data, allowing you to analyze past energy production trends and compare them with real-time data.

What formats can I download my data in?

You can download your solar energy data in various formats, including CSV, Excel, and PDF, from the 'Reports' section of the platform.

Can I get alerts for performance issues?

Yes, you can set up email notifications or alerts within the portal to notify you of any performance issues or system downtimes.

Are there different levels of data access?

Yes, the portal offers different levels of data access based on your account type. You may have restricted access or full access depending on your subscription.
